Welcome to Besant technologies

Technologies is a well-known IT training and placement institute in India, especially in Chennai and Bangalore, run by BDreamz Global Solutions Pvt. Ltd. since around 2009. It offers a wide range of classroom and online courses in areas such as Data Science, Python, Full-Stack Development, Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Software Testing. The institute highlights hands-on training with real-time projects, flexible batch timings, free demo classes, and placement assistance including resume preparation and mock interviews. While it is popular and affordable, the quality of training and placement support can vary by trainer and branch, so prospective students are advised to check reviews and clarify job-guarantee conditions before enrolling.
